Get started with open reproducible science!

Open reproducible science makes scientific methods, data and outcomes available to everyone. That means that everyone who wants should be able to find, read, understand, and run your workflows for themselves.

Image from https://www.earthdata.nasa.gov/esds/open-science/oss-for-eso-workshops

Few if any science projects are 100% open and reproducible (yet!). However, members of the open science community have developed open source tools and practices that can help you move toward that goal. You will learn about many of those tools in the Intro to Earth Data Science textbook. Dont worry about learning all the tools at once weve picked a few for you to get started with.
